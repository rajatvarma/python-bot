What is Strix?

Strix is a Gurgaon based startup launched in 2017, that was created by the efforts of the renowned architect and environmental activist Taylor Sharma. After having many sucessful projects, Strix is a pround recipient of the ECO-FRIENDLY CONSTRUCTERS award given by the UN.



Even though Strix has grown from a small startup to a large manufacturer, it has not affected the quality of our products and the integrity of our organisation. We will always be commited to making your home / office as environment friendly as possible.

Our Range Of New Eco-Friendly Products:

Geopolymer     + _

Geopolymer is an adhesive that can used as an alternative to cement, which also offers resistance against corrosion and damage from fires, water and CO2 exposure.
Geopolymer uses micro-polymer adhesive technology, developed at Strix, which makes it super strong and nearly unbreakable. Geopolymer also reduces the amount of water consumed, while affecting neither its consitency nor its efficiency as an adhesive.

HelioWindows     + _

HelioWindows are panels of glass with an attached layer of ultra thin solar cell film.

These panels of glass are made to order and can be used for buildings with heights upto 200m.

The film serves two functions, blocking out excess sunlight, and using the sunlight for generating electricity.

Each solar cell can generate upto 250MW of electricity, because of the new double mirror heat trapping technology, developed at Strix.

SynWood     + _

Plywood is one the most used construction materials nowadays. Each building has about 10 tonnes of plywood in it. That means about 1000 trees are cut for each construction project.

Strix has developed a new synthetic mix of organic materials, a plywood alternative, providing better strength along with protection against damage by water, termites and fires.

This would not only save millions of trees, but also provide a better aesthetic appeal to the building, allowed by easy customization and hassle-free polishing.
